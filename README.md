# 🚀 HireReady AI

> **Ace Every Interview with AI-Powered Personalized Coaching.**

HireReady AI is a production-grade AI Interview Coach designed to help students, fresh graduates, and professionals prepare for technical, behavioral, and HR interviews through personalized, AI-driven mock interview sessions.

Unlike traditional interview platforms, HireReady AI understands your **resume**, **target job description**, **company**, and **previous interview performance** to generate realistic interview experiences and actionable feedback.
